Game of PIG

1. Two players taking turn rolling the dice
2. After each roll, the player decides either to roll again or to pass
3. If either die is a “one”, it’s automotically the opponent’s turn
4. If both dice are “one” (snake eyes), player’s score is zeroed out
5. The first player to get 30 points or higher wins
